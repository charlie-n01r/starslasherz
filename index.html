Who I Am

Hi there! My name is Meitel Bolanos and I am a Mexican artist currently living in Canada. I have a passion for vivid storytelling as well as making new and exciting worlds come to life. Via the art of background design, I believe it is possible to bring out the true personality of a story while setting the tone for what the spirit of it should entail.

Some of my interests include cartoons, video games, horror films and exploring different food & entertainment spots around my city!
